**Idea 1: Ancient Greek Organic Minimalism**

- **Design Movement**: Neoclassical Wellness — inspired by ancient Greek philosophy of "Εὖ ζῆν" (the good life), blended with contemporary organic minimalism.
- **Core Principles**: Serenity through restraint; natural textures over digital flatness; asymmetric balance; reverence for whitespace as breath.
- **Color Philosophy**: Sage green (#8aab9e) as the primary tone — evoking nature, calm, and healing. Warm coral-salmon (#e07a5f) as the accent — a human warmth that contrasts the cool sage. Off-white (#faf8f5) backgrounds with warm undertones. Deep charcoal (#2d2d2d) for text.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Alternating asymmetric two-column layouts. Hero is full-bleed with centered text on textured sage. Sections shift left/right alignment to create visual rhythm. Services use a horizontal strip with icon-above-text cards.
- **Signature Elements**: Mandala/lotus SVG decorative dividers between sections. Subtle grain texture overlay on hero and CTA sections. Thin horizontal rule ornaments with a central lotus motif.
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Gentle fade-in-up animations on scroll. Buttons with soft border that fills on hover. Images with subtle scale on hover.
- **Animation**: Framer Motion scroll-triggered fade-up for each section. Hero text staggered entrance. Service cards slide in from sides.
- **Typography System**: Cormorant Garamond (serif, elegant) for display headings — wide letter-spacing for the main title. Lato (clean sans-serif) for body text and navigation. Coral accent text in italic Cormorant for taglines.

## Chosen Approach: **Idea 1 — Ancient Greek Organic Minimalism**

This approach best honors the original site's sage green palette, mandala decorative elements, coral accent color, and the philosophical "Εὖ ζῆν" theme. It elevates the existing aesthetic with refined typography (Cormorant Garamond + Lato), asymmetric layouts, and scroll-triggered animations while remaining faithful to the wellness center's identity.
